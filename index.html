About Deadpool

Deadpool is an antihero ficttional from Marvel Comics...

Deadpool, also known as Wade Wilson, is a fictional character appearing in Marvel Comics. He made his first appearance in The New Mutants #98 in February 1991. Deadpool is known for his sarcastic humor, unpredictable nature, and regenerative healing factor, which allows him to recover from virtually any injury. He is often portrayed as an antihero, taking on both heroic and villainous roles depending on the situation. With his red and black costume, dual katanas, and his trademark chimichangas, Deadpool has become a fan-favorite character known for breaking the fourth wall and delivering witty one-liners.
